Oyakodon

The recipe will teach you how to make coffee using a pour over method.

Ingredients

  • 14g of coffee
  • 238ml of water

Steps

  1. Bring your water to a boil.
  2. Place your pour over cone over a cup and insert your coffee filter.
  3. In the coffee filter add your ground coffee.
  4. Pour 30g of water over the ground coffee and allow to bloom for one minute.
  5. After the minute has passed add the rest of the hot water taking care that the water does not overfill the edges of the pour over cone.
