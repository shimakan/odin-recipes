Miso Soup

The recipe will teach you how to make miso soup, a traditional Japanese soup that starts with a dashi stock and is flavored with miso paste.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups of water
  • Dashi granules and miso paste
  • Tofu
  • Green onions

Steps

  1. Bring the water and dashi granules to a boil.
  2. Reduce the heat and whisk in miso paste.
  3. Stir in the tofu and green onions.
  4. Simmer and serve
